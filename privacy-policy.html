Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Global Mood is designed to be used without accounts and without personal identification.

What we collect

  • Anonymous emotion entries (emotion type and general area)
  • Timestamps related to shared emotions
  • Anonymous app usage statistics

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or personal identifiers.

Location data

  • Location is used only to display emotions on the global map
  • We do not store precise location data
  • Location information is intentionally generalized before use
  • Map visibility is limited to prevent identifying exact places

Emotion data

  • Shared emotions are anonymous
  • Emotion data automatically expires after a limited period
  • Older emotions gradually fade from the map

Personal reflections are stored only on your device and are never uploaded.

How we use data

We use anonymous data only to:

  • Show global emotional patterns
  • Display real-time participation
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Support a sense of shared emotional presence

We do not sell or share data with third parties.

Your control

  • You can revoke location access at any time in device settings
  • You can delete individual emotion entries
  • No account is required to use the app

Data security

We apply technical and design measures to reduce identifiability and protect anonymity.
